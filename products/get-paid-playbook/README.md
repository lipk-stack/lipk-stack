# The Get-Paid Playbook — sellable digital product

`playbook.md` is the full product: a practical guide of copy-paste scripts and
templates that help freelancers/small businesses set payment terms, write
invoices that get paid on sight, and chase late payments politely.

A formatted copy also lives in the owner's **Google Drive**
("The Get-Paid Playbook — sellable product — export to PDF"), ready to export.

## How this makes money (indirect: one-time product sales + funnel)

Unlike tips/affiliates (which need a steady traffic firehose), a digital product
can earn from a single share. The live sales/funnel page is already built at
`projects/get-paid-playbook/` and deployed with the site.

**To switch it on (≈5 min, all free):**
1. In Google Drive open the playbook doc → **File → Download → PDF Document**.
   (Optionally tidy the formatting / add a cover first.)
2. Create a free **Gumroad** or **Lemon Squeezy** account → new product → upload
   the PDF → set a price (suggested **$7–15**). They take a cut only on a sale;
   listing is free.
3. Paste the product link into `projects/get-paid-playbook/config.js` → `buyUrl`.
   The live page swaps its "coming soon" note for a real Buy button automatically.
4. Share the page once where freelancers gather (r/freelance, r/smallbusiness,
   Indie Hackers, X). The page also doubles as SEO content that funnels readers
   to the free invoice generator.

## Why it's a strong indirect path
- **You already have the matching audience surface** (InvoiceLite + its traffic).
- **Low refund risk, high perceived value:** scripts that recover a single late
  invoice pay for the guide many times over.
- **Reusable:** the same content can be a paid product *or* a free lead magnet to
  build an email list you can monetize later.

Real payout still requires your Gumroad/bank account (KYC) — that one signup is
the only step that can't be automated.
