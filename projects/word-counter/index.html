Frequently asked questions

How does the word counter count words?

It splits your text on spaces and line breaks and counts the non-empty chunks, so a hyphenated word like “well-known” counts as one word. Counts update live as you type or paste.

Is my text uploaded or stored anywhere?

No. The counter runs entirely in your browser and nothing is sent to a server. Your text is autosaved only to your own device’s local storage so it survives a refresh, and you can clear it any time.

How is reading and speaking time estimated?

Reading time assumes about 200 words per minute and speaking time about 130 words per minute, which are common averages for adults. Use them as a guide for essays, speeches and blog posts.
