Frequently asked questions

    

      How do I make an invoice for free?
      
Fill in your details, your client’s
        details and your line items, then click Download PDF. InvoiceLite builds a clean,
        professional invoice or quote entirely in your browser — no sign-up, no watermark and no cost.

    

    

      Is my invoice data private?
      
Yes. Everything runs in your browser
        and your data is saved only on your own device using local storage. Nothing is uploaded to a server,
        so your client and pricing information never leaves your computer.

    

    

      Can I create quotes and receipts too?
      
Yes. Switch the document Type
        to Invoice, Quote/Estimate or Receipt. You can set the currency, tax, a flat discount, due date and
        payment notes, and the live preview is exactly what downloads as a PDF.
